About Us

Energy Insight is an educational initiative designed to help Australians understand and reduce household energy consumption. We focus on practical, data-driven tips that can make a real difference.

This project was built as a demonstration of modern web technologies — using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — to create an interactive, easy-to-navigate website.

Our content is based on publicly available information from trusted sources such as the Energy Rating Program by the Australian Government.

Our Mission:

  • Educate households on how energy ratings work.
  • Encourage the purchase of energy-efficient appliances.
  • Support Australia's efforts to reduce greenhouse gas emissions.

Whether you are a student, homeowner, or just curious about energy use, we hope this site inspires you to make informed decisions and take action.
