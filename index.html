Welcome to Energy Insight

This site helps you explore how appliances consume energy in Australian households. Learn how choosing energy-efficient products can save money and reduce environmental impact.

Australia uses a national Energy Rating Label to help consumers compare the efficiency of products such as televisions, refrigerators, and air conditioners. The label shows a simple star rating — the more stars, the more efficient the appliance.

Did you know that energy use by appliances and equipment accounts for nearly 30% of the average Australian household electricity bill? Choosing energy-efficient appliances can save hundreds of dollars over the life of the product.

This website demonstrates:

  • How energy efficiency labels work and what the stars mean.
  • Trends in television energy use, including the effect of screen size and technology.
  • Practical tips for reducing household electricity consumption.

Our goal is to make energy information simple and actionable — empowering you to make better choices for your wallet and for the planet.
